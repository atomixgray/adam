📜 Decree Overview

Summary: The Italian government passed a new citizenship decree on March 27, 2025, impacting jure sanguinis cases. This includes limiting eligibility through 1948 maternal lines and introducing new application requirements.

  • 🔹 Decree Issued: March 27, 2025
  • 🔹 Grace Period End: June 27, 2025
  • 🔹 Official Decree (Gazzetta Ufficiale)

⚖️ Legal Strategies

  • 📌 Unconstitutionality Claims: Legal teams argue the decree violates equality principles in the Italian Constitution.
  • 📌 Administrative Filing Loopholes: Cases filed before the March 27 deadline are treated under old law.
  • 📌 Legal Teams: Law firms like ICA (Italian Citizenship Assistance), Studio Legale Vizzino, and Mazzeschi Law are involved in challenges.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

  • 🔍 Q: Can I still file a 1948 case?
    A: You can file under old law if your case is docketed before June 27, 2025.
  • 🔍 Q: Does my court date need to be before June 27?
    A: No, your case must only be filed and accepted before that date.
  • 🔍 Q: Can this decree be challenged?
    A: Yes, legal teams are filing constitutional appeals.
